How ExamSense AI Works

Transform your lecture materials into comprehensive exam questions in three simple steps

Step 1

Upload Lecture Audio or Transcript

Start by uploading your lecture recording in MP3, WAV, or any common audio format. Alternatively, paste your existing lecture transcript or study notes directly into the text field.

Supported: Audio files up to 500MB, text transcripts, lecture notes, textbook excerpts, and study guides

Step 2

AI Transcribes & Understands Content

Our advanced AI uses natural language processing to transcribe audio files with high accuracy, then analyzes the content to identify key concepts, important details, and potential exam topics.

AI Features: Automatic transcription, context understanding, topic identification, difficulty assessment, and relevance scoring

Step 3

Exam Questions Are Generated

ExamSense AI generates comprehensive exam questions in your preferred format: multiple choice, essay questions, or short answers. Each question comes with a detailed answer explanation to help you study effectively.

Output Options: Multiple choice questions (MCQ), essay questions, short answer questions, mixed format, exportable as PDF
